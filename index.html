Tim Bemers-Lee

The Inventor of HTML


Here's a time line of Tim Berners-Lee's life.

  • 1995- Born in London,England

  • 1973-Graduated from Queen's college, Oxford.

  • 1980-Started working at CERN as a freelance consultant for the physics research facility

  • 1989-Wrote his first proposal for the World Wide Web

  • 1990-Started working on the World Wide Web

  • 1991-Created his First Website

  • 1994-World Wide Web consortium was established

  • 1999-Appeared on Time's 100 Most influential list

  • 2004-Professor of computer science & later knighted by the order of the british empire

  • 2009-Started working for the UK Government & later launchd the World Wide Web Foundation

  • 2012-Became the President of Open Data Institute

  • 2013-Alliance for Affordable Internet launched

  • 2016-Received the 2016 ACM Award for inventing the World Wide Web

  • 2018-Announces new open-source startup Inrupt to give users more control over their personal data

  • 2021-The web's source code was auctioned by Sotheby's as a non fungible token (NFT) selling for US$5,434,500
